Chicken Biriyani

Description

Chicken Biriyani is a popular Indian dish which consists of flavored spiced rice with succulent meat. It is available across india in various varieties. The most popular ones are hyderabadi chicken biriyani and lucknowi chicken biriyani. Other variants of this dish are malabar, dindugal, kolkata and donne.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice -Long-grain basmati rice is traditionally used to make biryani.
  • Chicken - Traditionally, whole bone-in chicken pieces are used to make biryani.
  • Whole Spices - shahi jeera, bay leaves, star anise, cloves, and green cardamom pods.
  • Ground Spices - turmeric, Kashmiri red chili powder, Biryani Masala.
  • Onion - thinly sliced yellow onion caramelized in ghee makes for the signature Birista onions that are a must in the marinade as well as for topping.
  • Ghee - homemade ghee helps bring authentic taste and aromas to Biryani
  • Herbs - fresh mint and cilantro add a perfect combination of herbs for cool, citrusy flavors and a pop of color.

Steps

  1. Clean and wash the chicken thoroughly.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, salt, and garam masala. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 20–30 minutes.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot and fry sliced onions until golden brown. Remove some for garnish.
  5. Add the marinated chicken to the pot and cook until partially done.
  6. In another pot, boil water with whole spices (cardamom, cloves, bay leaf, cinnamon) and salt. Add the soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain the water.
  7. Layer the partially cooked chicken and rice alternately in the pot.
  8. Sprinkle fried onions, chopped mint, coriander leaves, and saffron milk over the layers.
  9. Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low flame (dum) for 20–25 minutes.
  10. Gently mix the layers and serve hot with raita or salad.
