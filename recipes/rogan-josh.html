Rogan Josh

Description

Rogan Josh is a traditional Kashmiri curry made with tender pieces of mutton cooked in a flavorful gravy of yogurt and aromatic spices. Known for its rich red color and deep flavors, this dish pairs perfectly with steamed rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton or lamb pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan and add whole spices.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add mutton pieces and sear until lightly browned.
  5. Mix in chili powder, turmeric, coriander, cumin, and salt.
  6. Add whisked yogurt slowly, stirring continuously to prevent curdling.
  7. Add water, cover, and simmer until the meat is tender.
  8. Cook until the gravy thickens and oil separates.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or naan.
