Aloo Paratha

Description

Aloo Paratha is a popular North Indian stuffed bread made with wheat flour dough and a flavorful mashed potato filling. Cooked with ghee or butter, it is commonly served with yogurt, pickle, or a dollop of butter and makes a hearty breakfast or meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or butter for roasting

Steps

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes in a bowl.
  2. Add onions, green chilies, coriander, cumin powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Knead wheat flour with water and salt to form a soft dough. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
  4. Take a dough ball and roll into a small circle.
  5. Place potato filling in the center and seal the edges.
  6. Roll gently into a round paratha.
  7. Cook on a hot tawa with ghee or butter until golden brown on both sides.
  8. Serve hot with curd, pickle, or butter.
