Masala Dosa

Description

Masala Dosa is a South Indian delicacy consisting of a crispy fermented rice and lentil crepe filled with a spiced mashed potato filling. It is typically served with coconut chutney and sambar, making it a perfect breakfast or dinner dish loved all over India.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Steps

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 4â€“6 hours.
  2. Grind both into a smooth batter, mix well, and let it ferment overnight or for 8 hours.
  3. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  4. In a pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds, curry leaves, onions, green chilies, and turmeric.
  5. Add mashed potatoes and salt, then mix to form the masala filling.
  6. Heat a tawa, pour a ladle of batter, and spread thinly in a circular motion.
  7. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook till golden brown.
  8. Place some potato masala in the center and fold the dosa.
  9. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
